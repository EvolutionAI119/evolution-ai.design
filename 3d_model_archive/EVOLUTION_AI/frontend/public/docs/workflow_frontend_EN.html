2.3 Parameter Groups & Constraints
Dimension (9) Determines vehicle envelope and spatial layout; primary input to deriveHardpoints().
Styling (6) Controls body surface characteristics (pillar angles, waistline, shoulder); affects sideProfile() and topWidthProfile().
Advanced (6) Fine-tunes local styling details (hood slope, tumblehome, rear taper); applied as local surface deformation.

Hardpoint Derivation Rules:
frontAxleX = FO - L/2
rearAxleX = L/2 - RO
hoodHeight = GC + WR + hoodSlope*FO
roofPeak = H - GC
beltLineY = WL
deckLidY = GC + WR*deckRatio
Total derived: 48 secondary hardpoints, latency <0.1ms.
